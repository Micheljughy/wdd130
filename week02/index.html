Congo

The Republic of Congo, also known as Congo-Brazzaville, is a country located in Central Africa. It is bordered by Gabon to the west, Cameroon and the Central African Republic to the north, the Democratic Republic of the Congo to the east and south, and the Atlantic Ocean to the southwest. The capital city is Brazzaville, which is situated on the Congo River directly across from Kinshasa, the capital of the Democratic Republic of the Congo.
